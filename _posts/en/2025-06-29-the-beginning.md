You made it.

I don't know how, I don't know from where, but here you are, reading the first post on this blog.

That says something about you. I'm not sure what exactly, but something.

---

You know, there's something strange about beginnings. They never start when you think they do. This blog existed for months as an empty folder, a configured domain, and a vague promise to "write more." The classic infrastructure for procrastination.

And yet, here we are.

You reading. Me having finally written something.

---

I don't have big revelations for this first post. No manifesto, no promises of consistency. It's just this — a space that now exists, where nothing existed before, and that will keep existing even if nobody reads it.

After all, writing was always a slightly strange conversation with people you haven't met yet.

---

Thanks for making it this far.

Talk soon.
